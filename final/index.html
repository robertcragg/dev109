DEV-109 Portfolio (Spring 2024)

This website is my final project for my intro to web development class at Bellevue College.
It is a culmination of all the things I learned throughout the quarter about: CSS/HTML/JavaScript.

Even though I am still very new to the web development space, I did my best to put together a website that has some interesting design elements, and that shows off some of the different concepts that I learned along the way. It is by no means perfect, and it definitely isn't as responsive as I would have liked. However, I gave it my best shot, and I am proud of what I was able to put together in such a short amount of time.

Thanks for stopping by. I hope you were able to get to know me a little better, and enjoyed seeing a snapshot of my learning journey over the last several months.
